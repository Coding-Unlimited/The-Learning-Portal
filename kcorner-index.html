About

Kiddies corner is all about empowering children to reach new frontiers of development through engaging learning practices. Various quizzes, learning games and many other engaging learning activities.

There is a lot more to life. Don't just learn, experience. "Don't just read, absorb. Don't just change, transform. Don't just relate, advocate. Don't just promise, prove. Don't just criticize, encourage. Don't just think, ponder. Don't just take, give. Don't just see, feel. Don’t just dream, do. Don't just hear, listen. Don't just talk, act. Don't just tell, show. Don't just exist, live.” - Roy T. Bennett

This is why at the Learning Portal we believe in learning through experience, absorption, transformation, advocacy, proven methods, encouraging individuality and pondering beyond the obvious.
